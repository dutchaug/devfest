  • QAFE

    QAFE, short for Quality Application Framework for Enterprises, was established in 2008 by Paul Mehilal and Rokesh Jankie as a software division of Qualogy. Challenged by the dynamic nature and short lifecycle of technology frameworks, Rokesh started engineering a new platform based on Google Web Toolkit that would enable the development of applications independently of back and front ends.

    QAFE allows you to recycle legacy applications, build applications from scratch and execute your mobile strategy to an anywhere, anyplace collaboration. It also offers a one-of-a-kind Oracle Forms modernization tool, that lets you unlock legacy applications to any modern frontend of your choice.

    As of 2013, QAFE has offices in the US, Europe and South America, and services clients on all continents.
