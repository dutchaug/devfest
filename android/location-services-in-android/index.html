Location Services in Android

Room: darkroom Time: 13:20 - 14:00
Language: English Level: Intermediate

Speaker: Thijs Damen

Tweet
  • Android 5
  • Google Location Services 1

The talk will focus about the do's and don'ts about the Google Location Services. Giving the audience an insight how location works on android and how your app can leverage all the tools available, including best practices for performance and battery life. Different approaches will be shown such as the default LocationManager and the GPS location APIs.

We will see different strategies for location, including passive updates, geofencing and giving the user what he wants, when he wants it.
