Layar on Google Glass

Room: darkroom Time: 10:40 - 11:20
Language: English Level: Beginner

Speaker: Ronald van der Lingen

Tweet
  • Android 5
  • Google Glass 1

Google Glass is an interesting new platform that brings many opportunities and challenges for developers. As one of the lucky few Glass Explorers, I have been busy developing several prototypes of the Layar Augmented Reality technology on Glass.

In this talk, I will discuss the basics of Google Glass and the developer APIs currently available (Mirror API and Android apps on Glass). I will present the lessons learned from our experimentation and will give some tips on Google Glass UI design.
