Arnd Brugman (Sogeti)

Tweet

As Innovator Arnd helps organisations with innovative trajects from initial Idea and Inspiration through Vision, Strategy and Roadmap until assistance with Proof of Concepts and Pilots. He has significant experience with Innovation trajects, service- and product development. Arnd is highly motivated, reliable and pro-active.

At Sogeti NL Arnd Brugman is (co) responsible for the themes Mobile Apps, Social Collaboration, Cross Channel, UX and the New World of Work.

Mobile Apps is a consumer trend that is taking over companies rapidly, both on the consumer external side and the internal workforce. Arnd is co-responsible for the proposition development of Sogeti NL and has acted as a coach and guest lecturer for the joint Sogeti NL/Nyenrode Business University "Apps in Business" research project.

A passion for Arnd is the true user centric approach in every communication, interaction and transaction. Man-Machine Interaction can make a whole new step by using more modern solutions (like Apps) and the use of the right means and channels (Mobile/Web/Social) the user prefers to enhance the User eXperience. By taking in account the place and time of interaction (Contextual Intention) together we create more intention aware solutions for every touchpoint.

Arnd is a contributor to The App Effect and the Connected Workforce and co-author of the book "TeamPark, from Crowd to Community" (ISBN 9789075414233) which describes the platform-independent method to grow your organisation a Social side. This powerful side is present in every organisation but isn't often made explicit and lacks management attention.

In his spare time he relaxes by diving in the Dutch and foreign waters. Arnd is a certified PADI dive-master which enables him to assist during various diving courses.

App Strategy Workshop

Room: laboratory Time: 10:40 - 12:10
Language: English Level: Beginner

In a short time this workshop gives you a concrete hands-on with building a fundament under a mobile strategy. With a good mobile app you can lift the productivity of personnel, efficiency of an organisation and satisfaction of customers and employees to a higher level. How do you make sure mobile choices are aligned to your company's strategy? And how do you keep the satisfaction of customers in clear focus with all the decisions that you make?

Together with Sogeti you find answers to all these questions in the App Strategy Workshop. We will guide you in making the right choices and treasuring your best ideas.

Arnd is co-hosting this workshop with Magdelijn Emaus
