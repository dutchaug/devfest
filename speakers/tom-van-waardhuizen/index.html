I run global developer programs for Google's Cloud platform. Focusing on developer communities, startups and incubators, technical and marketing communication, business and organizational strategy, and team development.

What's happening with Google Cloud Platform

Room: lightroom Time: 13:20 - 14:00
Language: English Level: Beginner

Google Cloud Platform provides world-class PaaS and IaaS offerings, in the form of Google App Engine and Google Compute Engine. We also provide robust data analysis tools that allow you to process terabytes of data in seconds.

This talk provides an overview of Google Cloud Platform services as well as the developer programs that support them.
