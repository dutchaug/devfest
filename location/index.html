Venue: ACE Venture Lab

Important note: ACE Venture Lab is located at Science Park 105, however, the main entrance for the DevFest will be:

Science Park 107 (UvA)

Map and directions (If you come by car you can best park your care at P3. Parking is at your own cost at €2,40 per hour or €17,20 per day, or if you loose your parking ticket).

When we first visited ACE Venture Lab (ACE) at Science Park Amsterdam we were impressed with the atmosphere and ambience of this location. For example ACE holds office in the same building as Nikhef (the National Institute for Subatomic Physics). When we walked in the lobby we saw all kinds of measurement systems, including a monitor with a live link displaying what project was currently running on the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at CERN! "Did you know Python was invented here?" Jeroen van Duffelen, manager at ACE, asked us. "And did you know the first European internet connection saw the light of day in this building?". An inspiring environment. Perfect for hosting a DevFest!

ACE Venture Lab actively supports you in building a successful technology or science based company.

Do you have a business idea or have you already started a company? We tailor our program and facilities to support you at every stage.

Building a company and want to take it to the next level?
Get hands-on support through active individual mentoring and expert training. Become part of our network and benefit from introductions to business opportunities, investors, and a pool of talented people. During the program you can use shared financial and legal services, and rent affordable office or lab space that provides all the needed facilities.

Interested in entrepreneurship or starting a business?
Learn the basics of starting a company during our Explore Program. As a participant of the Explore Program you can join weekly workshops, take part in group mentoring sessions, and have access to a help desk for all your questions about legal, financial and intellectual property issues. During the program you can use shared office spaces to work among like minded people and learn from startups that are part of our community.
