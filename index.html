GDG DevFest NL 2013

Tweet

Welcome to the first Google DevFest in The Netherlands!

The GDG Dutch Android User Group and GDG Netherlands join forces in bringing you a single day event on Saturday October 19th 2013 in the style of a true Google DevFest. This means interesting talks and code labs, presented by national and international leaders using Google technology, in an informal setting, an inspirational environment with lots of room for open discussion, a barcamp and time to meet during the breaks or the After Party.

Google Cloud, web and mobile technologies

National and international speakers will tell you about their experiences with Google Cloud, web and mobile technologies like Android, AngularJS, App Engine, Google Glass, Go and more. Check out the program!
